Ilahia College of Engineering and Technology, Muvattupuzha was established in the year 2002 under ILAHIA Trust. It is affiliated to APJ Abdul Kalam Kerala Technological University (KTU) and Mahatma Gandhi University, Kottayam and approved by All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi. The college offers B.Tech at undergraduate level, M.Tech, MBA and MCA at postgraduate level. Admission to same is done on the basis of entrance exams.
